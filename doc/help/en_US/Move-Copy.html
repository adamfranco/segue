How to move/copy

Moving or Copying a component (section, page, content block) of a site elsewhere in the site or to another site is an easy process:

  1. Go to the edit-mode view of the site that displays the component you wish to copy.
  2. Click the '+ Selection' link for the component.
    • In 'New Mode' the + Selection link is in the options-bar that appears when you mouse-over the component.
    • In 'Classic Mode' the + Selection link is next to the edit and delete links.
  3. Browse to the Page Container or Content Container that where you wish to move/copy the component to.
  4. Click the '+ Move/Copy' link.
  5. In the dialog panel that pops up choose the options you desire, check the components that you would like to copy from the list at the bottom of the dialog, and click the 'Copy Checked >>' button.

Modes

Copy

This mode will insert copies of the checked blocks/pages at the destination. The originals will not be changed.

Move

Move the checked blocks/pages to the destination. Links to them will now land at their new location. They will no longer be availible in their old location.
